Earned Schedule Tool


    Initial consideration
  • Traditionally SPI does not consider the critical path.
  • A bigger problem is when project slips in time and SPI does not provide a clear picture of the real status of the project.
  • Earned Schedule show effectively how much of the project has been done.

    Here is a tool to help you understand how to calculate Earned Schedule:
  • Let's say your project is planned to be completed in 6 months.
  • First, input the amount of money planned for each month (effort, it means, cost).
  • Then, input which month of develpment your project is at.
  • Finaly, input how much of work your project has accomplished.
  • Click Submit and see the results below.
